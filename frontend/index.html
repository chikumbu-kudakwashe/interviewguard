CV Support

InterviewGuard helps students prepare attachment-ready CVs by highlighting the key sections employers expect to see, including academic background, technical skills, projects, references, and relevant experience.

Personal Details
Full name, email, phone number, location, LinkedIn, and portfolio links.
Education
University, degree programme, year of study, key modules, and achievements.
Skills
Programming languages, frameworks, tools, databases, and soft skills.
Projects
Include 2–4 strong projects with short descriptions and GitHub/demo links.
Attachment CV Tip
Keep your CV clear, one to two pages long, and tailored to the company you are applying to. Focus on what you can do, the tools you have used, and the projects that prove your skills.

About InterviewGuard
A lightweight interview readiness platform built for students, graduates, and job seekers preparing for online and physical interviews.
Built for Zimbabwean attachment students

Interview preparation, protected by smart readiness tools.

InterviewGuard helps users prepare confidently by combining interview practice, CV guidance, and network checks for smoother online interviews.
What InterviewGuard Does
Network Readiness
Tests download, upload, latency, and interview success probability.
Interview Guard
Monitors connection drops and prepares an apology alert when needed.
Interview Prep Bank
Provides common interview questions across faculties and difficulty levels.
CV Support
Guides students on what to include in an attachment-ready CV.
Why It Exists

Many students lose interview confidence because of poor network conditions, limited preparation, or uncertainty about what employers expect. InterviewGuard was created to reduce that pressure by giving users practical tools before, during, and after the interview process.

Privacy First
Your information stays in your control
Profile details are stored locally in your browser and are only used to personalise interview alerts, apology previews, and readiness features.
